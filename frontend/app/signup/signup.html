SENY(Sharing Economy Near You)

What is SENY?

  • SENY is an appication that enables users to take part in a sharing economy!
  • Users of SENY are able to upload products and create advertisements for their products.
  • Other users will then respond to the advertisement and rent the product!
  • Want to rent something that isn't listed? Create a demand advertisement!

Features:

Reusable Products

Most of the information needed to create an advertisement is uploaded into what's called a product. This allows for easily recreating advertisements!

Easy Image Uploading

Images can be uploaded and shared between products. Each product has it's own set of images and one display image. This allows you to show your prospective clients what you've got!

In App Messaging

No need to share your email or phone number to communicate with your clients!

Review

The ability to review products will help you gain prestige while giving insight into the kind of people you are dealing with.
